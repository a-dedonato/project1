Level 1

Welcome to improv! In this course, you'll focus on the basic principles and components of good improv. Class will consist mostly of simple activities that will get you connected to your classmates and more comfortable being goofy around each other.

Prerequisites: None!

Sign Up

Level 2

Here we'll transition from learning about the principles of good improv to learning about how to apply those principles to scenes (i.e., pretending you are a character interacting other characters with a beginning, middle, and end)

Prerequisites: Level 1 or other improv experience

Sign Up

Level 3

In Level 3, we'll focus entirely on scenework. We'll also learn how to put a show together and will end the term with a Kellogg-only performance at the iO Theater.

Prerequisites: Level 2 or other scenework-driven improv experience
